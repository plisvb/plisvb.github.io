What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your event, you are required to notify us at least seven (7) days in advance.

What clothing should I wear?

Closed-toe shoes are mandatory, following our establishment's rules.

For safety reasons, you must wear comfortable closed-toe sports shoes that fully cover your feet.

If you're wearing open-toed or open-top shoes, such as beach sandals, ballet flats, or lightweight fabric shoes, you won't be allowed to throw. High heels are also discouraged.

We also recommend wearing loose and comfortable shirts that won't restrict your arm or upper body movements.

What is the minimum age?

The minimum age is 10 years and older when accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Can I come with a child or a spectator?

Young participants can engage in axe throwing from the age of 10, as long as they are accompanied by an adult.

It is not prohibited to bring a child under 10 years of age, but we discourage it.

Our facilities are designed for an informed audience and can, therefore, become very noisy.

Spectators are welcome, but they cannot participate in axe throwing.

Can I use my own axe?

Participants must use the axes provided by our staff.

Can I drop in at any time to give it a try?

Walk-ins are accepted if we are already open for other reservations.
